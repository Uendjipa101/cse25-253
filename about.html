About Us

Learn more about who we are and what drives us

Kanaandjoze Holdings (Pty) Ltd is a 100% citizen owned fully registered company lead by highly qualified and trained professionals who are passionate about keeping your space clean and pest-free so you can live and work comfortably

Who We are

Kanaandjoze Holdings is a professional pest control and cleaning services company based in Botswana. Established in 2017, we have grown into a trusted name in pest management and cleaning solutions, serving both residential and commercial clients across the country.

We take pride in delivering high quality, reliable and affordable services that give our clients peace of mind. Whether it's eliminating pests or keeping your space spotless, our team is dedicated to getting the job done right every time.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide safe, effective and affordable pest control and cleaning services that protect the health and comfort of our clients. We are committed to using eco-friendly products and professional methods that deliver real results without harming people, pets or the environment.

Our Vision

To become the leading pest control and cleaning services company in Botswana, known for our professionalism, reliability and commitment to customer satisfaction.
